When to Re-Roof vs Repair

Re-roofing makes sense when:

  • Age of roof – Tile roofs over 40-50 years, metal roofs over 25-30 years typically need replacement
  • Widespread damage – Multiple leaks, extensive rust, or structural issues across the roof
  • Repeated repairs – If you're constantly patching issues, replacement provides better long-term value
  • Energy efficiency upgrade – Modern Colorbond® roofs with proper insulation dramatically reduce cooling costs
  • Preparing to sell – A new roof adds significant value and buyer appeal
  • Planning major renovations – Replace the roof before other major work to avoid access issues later

Re-Roofing Process Outline

Our systematic approach ensures quality results:

  • Site assessment & quote – Thorough inspection, material recommendations, and detailed pricing
  • Council permits – We handle all necessary building permits and approvals (typically required for re-roofing)
  • Safe removal – Complete removal of existing roof, sarking, and damaged timber (disposed of responsibly)
  • Structural repairs – Replace any damaged battens, rafters, or fascia boards
  • New sarking & insulation – Install modern breathable sarking and insulation for energy efficiency
  • New roof installation – Premium Colorbond® steel or quality concrete/terracotta tiles installed to manufacturer specs
  • Flashings & gutters – New valleys, ridges, flashings, and gutters for complete weather protection
  • Clean-up & inspection – Full site clean-up and final inspection before handover

Council Permits & Compliance

Building permits are typically required for re-roofing work in Sorrento. We manage the entire permit process including engineering specifications, wind rating calculations, and council submissions. All work complies with current Australian Building Codes and local regulations.
