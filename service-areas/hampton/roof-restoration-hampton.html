What's Included in Roof Restoration

Our comprehensive restoration service includes:

  • Thorough inspection and assessment – We identify all issues including cracked tiles, rusted fasteners, deteriorated bedding and pointing
  • Complete cleaning – High-pressure or soft-wash cleaning (depending on roof type) to remove moss, lichen, dirt and organic matter
  • Repairs – Replace broken tiles, repair valley flashings, fix ridge caps, and address any structural issues
  • Repointing and rebedding – For tile roofs, we remove old deteriorated mortar and apply flexible modern bedding and pointing compounds
  • Primer application – Quality primers seal the surface and ensure maximum coating adhesion
  • Protective membrane coatings – Premium roof coatings (typically 2 coats) that seal, waterproof, and add years to your roof's life
  • Gutter and valley treatment – Clean, repair and coat gutters and valleys for complete weather protection

Typical Timeline & Warranty

Most roof restoration projects in Hampton take 2-5 days depending on roof size, complexity, and weather conditions. We'll provide an accurate timeline during your free quote.

Warranty coverage: Our restoration work comes with comprehensive warranties covering both workmanship (typically 7-10 years) and premium coating products (manufacturer warranties up to 15 years). All warranty details are clearly outlined in your quote.
