About Us

We're local roofing specialists proudly servicing the Mornington Peninsula — from Mount Eliza to Sorrento. For over 25 years, our team has delivered expert roof repairs, restorations, re-roofing and roof painting for homes and businesses.

Our workmanship-first approach means clear diagnosis, transparent quoting and projects completed to Australian Standards. We use proven materials designed for coastal conditions and stand behind every job.

We value honesty, reliability and care in the details—protecting your home, improving street appeal and extending roof life.

  • Coastal-grade coatings
  • Australian Standards
  • Clean site handover
